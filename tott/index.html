ID Timer

Timer on the Tens is a highly customizable tool to remind ham radio operators that a station ID is due. WHY? FCC Rules and Regulations for Amateur Radio specifies that each station must identify every 10 minutes during a conversation.

On the Tens?

Many ham operators ID by watching the clock and 10, 20, 30 minutes past the hour is a convenient focus! Timer on the Tens is a tool that carries that on and reminds you to ID so that your titillating conversation doesn't distract you from your legal obligation to identify in a timely manner!
